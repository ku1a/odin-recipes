Curried Sausages

About this recipe

Sausages in a curry sauce with vegetables, served over a bed of rice. What's not to love?

Ingredients

  • 1/2 tbsp oil
  • 600g sausages (of your choice, I like pork)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 onion (halved and sliced)
  • 1 carrot (peeled, sliced on the diagonal)
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 3 tbsp flour (any)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 cup peas (frozen)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  2. Cook sausages, turning to brown all over then remove. Optional: slice sausages into pieces on the diagonal.
  3. In the same skillet, add garlic and onion. Cook for 2 minutes until translucent.
  4. Add curry powder and stir for 30 seconds. Add flour and mix for 30 seconds.
  5. Gradually pour in chicken stock, mixing constantly.
  6. Add carrots, sugar, salt and pepper, stir well.
  7. Add sausages and peas, bring to simmer and cook for 3 minutes or until sauce thickens.
  8. Serve over mashed potato, rice, pasta or noodles. For low carb, try mashed cauliflower!
